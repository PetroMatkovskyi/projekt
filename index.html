About Freddy Burger

Freddy Burger is a superior Burger Food Truck you can find roaming the streets of Lviv. We are a symbol of independence and premium budget food on the go. We cook our burgers using the freshest and the highest quality local ingredients.

If you want to arrange a budget party and love our gourmet burgers, you can book our truck and feed your guests with excellent street food.
